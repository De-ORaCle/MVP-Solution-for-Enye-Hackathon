Sybaris is an online web application that allow users to have access to medical care easily and quickly.

Sybaris solves three basic problems: Helping users find the nearest hospitals during emergencies, giving users access to virtual consultation concerning basic symptoms that the user may be experiencing and providing a pharmacy where the user can easily get normal medicines that are readily available in physical pharmacies.

- Helping users find the nearest hospitals during emergencies

In this case, Sybaris accesses your location and immediately shows you the nearest medical centre to you. Sybaris also be works on reccommending the best hospital for you if you are diagnosed with any disease during consultation. Sybaris can also help you call an ambulance for extreme emergencies.

- Access to virtual consultation

Sybaris provides you with virtual consultation with our doc-bot. Note that this consultation cannot be compared to the expertise of a real medical professional. For extreme medical cases, find the nearest hospital and consult with a real doctor. Virtual consultation are solely for minor symptoms that aren't extreme. Sybaris also allows you to contact a doctor if you wish to schedule a physical consultation

- Vrtual Pharmacy

Sybaris displays a virtual pharmacy that can contact the closest pharmacy to you and get the drug you want delivered to you as quickly as possible. Sybaris will use your location to access the closest pharmacy and display all the products they have so you can easily either get them yourselves or order for it.
