Sybaris is an online web application that allow users to have access to medical care easily and quickly.

Sybaris solves three basic problems: Helping users find the nearest hospitals during emergencies, giving users access to virtual consultation concerning basic symptoms that the user may be experiencing and providing a pharmacy where the user can easily get normal medicines that are readily available in physical pharmacies.

- Helping users find the nearest hospitals during emergencies

In this case, Sybaris accesses your location and immediately shows you the nearest medical centre to you. Sybaris also be works on reccommending the best hospital for you if you are diagnosed with any disease during consultation.
